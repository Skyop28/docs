Right Spot accepts major credit and debit cards through Stripe, a leading payment processor trusted by millions of businesses worldwide.

## Accepted cards

- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- Discover

Both credit and debit cards are accepted. Prepaid cards may also work depending on the issuer.

## Card security

Your card information is handled entirely by Stripe. When you enter your card details:

1. The information is encrypted in your browser
2. Sent directly to Stripe's secure servers
3. Verified and processed by Stripe
4. Right Spot receives only confirmation of payment

**Right Spot never has access to your full card number, expiration date, or CVC.** We receive only a token that represents the transaction, not your actual card details.

See [Payment security](/security/payment-security) for more information about how your financial data is protected.

## Processing time

Card payments typically process within seconds. You see the result immediately after clicking Pay (or after completing 3D Secure verification if required).

## Why cards may be declined

Cards can be declined for several reasons:

**Insufficient funds**: The card does not have enough available credit or balance.

**Fraud prevention**: Your bank flagged the transaction as potentially fraudulent. This is common for first-time purchases from new merchants.

**Card restrictions**: Some cards have restrictions on online purchases or international transactions.

**Incorrect details**: The card number, expiration date, or CVC was entered incorrectly.

**Card expired**: The card has passed its expiration date.

### If your card is declined

1. Verify all card details are entered correctly
2. Check with your bank if there are any holds or restrictions
3. Try a different card
4. Use an alternative payment method

## 3D Secure

3D Secure is an additional security layer that some banks require. When triggered, you are asked to verify the transaction through your bank's authentication system.

This may involve:
- Entering a one-time code sent to your phone
- Confirming through your banking app
- Answering security questions

If 3D Secure verification fails, the payment is not processed. You can retry or use a different payment method.

Refunds for card payments are returned to the original card. Processing time varies by bank but typically takes 5-10 business days to appear on your statement.
