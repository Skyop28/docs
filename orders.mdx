The Orders section provides a comprehensive view of all your purchases, including active orders awaiting delivery and your complete purchase history.

## Viewing your orders

Navigate to **Orders** in the sidebar to access your order list. Orders are displayed in reverse chronological order, with your most recent orders appearing first.

[IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Orders list view]

## Order information

Each order entry displays the following information:

**Reference number**: A unique identifier for the order, formatted as `ORD-` followed by a timestamp-based code. Use this reference when contacting support about a specific order.

**Pokecoin amount**: The quantity of Pokecoins purchased in this order.

**Total paid**: The payment amount in your selected currency.

**Status**: The current state of the order (see [tracking statuses](/tracking/statuses) for details).

**Date**: When the order was placed.

## Filtering and searching

For accounts with extensive order history, the orders page includes filtering options:

**Status filter**: View only orders with a specific status, such as delivered orders or pending payments.

**Date range**: Filter orders within a specific time period.

**Search**: Find orders by reference number or amount.

## Order details

Click any order to expand its details panel. The expanded view includes:

### Transaction information

- Payment method used
- Payment confirmation timestamp
- Transaction reference from the payment provider

### Delivery information

- Market ID where Pokecoins were delivered
- Delivery timestamp
- Delivery confirmation from the Poketwo system

### Timeline

A chronological log of all events related to the order, from placement through delivery. This timeline is useful for understanding when each step occurred and for troubleshooting any delays.

## Order actions

Depending on the order status, different actions may be available:

**Pending payment orders**: You can cancel the order or complete the payment if you navigated away before finishing.

**Delivered orders**: You can view the full delivery confirmation or report an issue if the Pokecoins were not received.

**Failed orders**: You can view the reason for failure and retry the order or contact support.

## Exporting order history

You can export your order history for your records. Click the export option at the top of the orders list to download a file containing your order data.
