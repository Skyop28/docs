Right Spot is a dedicated platform for purchasing Pokecoins, the virtual currency used in the Poketwo Discord bot. Since 2023, we have served thousands of customers worldwide, delivering billions of Pokecoins with a focus on speed, security, and reliability.

## What we do

We provide a straightforward way to purchase Pokecoins and have them delivered directly to your Poketwo account. Our automated delivery system ensures that most orders are fulfilled within seconds of payment confirmation.

## Why trainers choose Right Spot

**Speed**: Our delivery system processes orders automatically. Once your payment is confirmed, your Pokecoins are typically delivered within seconds through the Poketwo market system.

**Pricing**: We offer competitive rates starting at $0.11 per million Pokecoins, with additional discounts available for bulk purchases. Our pricing is transparent with no hidden fees.

**Payment flexibility**: We accept multiple payment methods including PayPal, credit and debit cards, UPI (for customers in India), and various cryptocurrencies. This ensures you can pay using your preferred method regardless of your location.

**Security**: Your payment information never passes through our servers. All transactions are processed through established payment providers like PayPal, Stripe, and Cashfree. We only store the information necessary to fulfill your order and provide customer support.

**Support**: Our support team responds to inquiries within 6 hours on average. You can reach us through our website or Discord server for any questions or issues.

## How delivery works

Right Spot uses the Poketwo market system to deliver your Pokecoins. When you place an order, you provide your market ID, which is a unique identifier tied to your Poketwo account. Once your payment is confirmed, our system automatically sends the Pokecoins to your market ID.

This method is the same system that players use to trade with each other through Poketwo, ensuring compatibility and reliability.
