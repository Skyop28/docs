The Settings section allows you to customize your Right Spot experience, manage notification preferences, and view your account information.

## Accessing settings

Navigate to **Settings** in the sidebar to access your account configuration options.

[IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Settings page view]

## Account information

The top of the settings page displays your account details pulled from your Discord authentication:

- **Username**: Your Discord username
- **Email**: The email address associated with your Discord account
- **Account created**: When you first signed in to Right Spot
- **Discord ID**: Your unique Discord identifier

<Note>
Account information is managed through your Discord account. To update your username or email, make the changes in Discord and they will reflect in Right Spot on your next sign-in.
</Note>

## Notification preferences

Control which notifications you receive and how they are delivered.

### Email notifications

**Order confirmations**: Receive an email when you place an order and when payment is confirmed. Enabled by default.

**Delivery notifications**: Receive an email when your Pokecoins are delivered. Enabled by default.

**Promotional emails**: Receive updates about discounts, new features, and announcements. You can opt out of promotional emails while keeping transactional notifications enabled.

### Dashboard notifications

Configure which events appear in your dashboard notification feed:

- Order status changes
- Payment confirmations
- Delivery completions
- Account alerts

## Display preferences

Customize how information is displayed in your dashboard:

**Currency display**: Select your preferred currency for displaying prices. This does not affect payment processing, only the display format.

**Time format**: Choose between 12-hour and 24-hour time formats for timestamps throughout the dashboard.

## Connected accounts

View information about your Discord connection:

- **Discord account**: Shows the currently connected Discord account
- **Connection status**: Indicates whether the connection is active
- **Last verified**: When the Discord connection was last validated

## Signing out

You can sign out of Right Spot from the settings page or by clicking the sign-out option in the sidebar. Signing out ends your current session but does not delete your account or order history.
