The Settings page displays your account information and allows you to manage your email preferences.

## Accessing settings

Navigate to **Settings** in the sidebar to access your account settings.

![IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Full screenshot of the Settings page](https://ik.imagekit.io/awnish90/sttings.png)

---

## Profile information

The first section displays your account details from your Discord authentication.

### Username

Your Discord username is displayed here. This is pulled directly from your Discord account and cannot be changed on Right Spot.

### Email address

The email address associated with your Discord account. This email is used for order confirmations and delivery notifications.

<Note>
To update your username or email, make the changes in your Discord account settings. The updates will reflect in Right Spot on your next sign-in.
</Note>

---

## Security

The security section shows your connected accounts.

### Connected accounts

Shows your Discord account connection status. Since Right Spot uses Discord for authentication, your Discord account will always show as connected when you are logged in.

---

## Email preferences

Control what promotional emails you receive from Right Spot.

![IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Email preferences section with toggle](https://ik.imagekit.io/awnish90/pref.png)

### Promotional emails

Use the toggle to enable or disable promotional emails. When enabled, you will receive:

- Updates about special deals and discounts
- New feature announcements
- Stock alerts and pricing updates

When disabled, you will only receive essential transactional emails such as order confirmations and delivery notifications.

<Note>
Transactional emails about your orders cannot be disabled. These are essential communications about your purchases.
</Note>

---

## Signing out

You can sign out of Right Spot by clicking the sign-out option in the sidebar. Signing out ends your current session but does not delete your account or order history.
