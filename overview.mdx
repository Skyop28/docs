The Overview is the first page you see when you log into your Right Spot dashboard. It provides a complete snapshot of your account activity, recent orders, and quick access to common actions.

## Accessing the overview

After signing in with Discord, you are automatically taken to the Overview page. You can return to this page at any time by:

- Clicking the Right Spot logo in the sidebar
- Clicking "Overview" in the sidebar navigation

## Statistics cards

At the top of the Overview, you will find statistics cards displaying your account metrics.

[IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Close-up of the statistics cards section]

### Total orders

This card shows the total number of orders you have placed since creating your account. This includes all order typesâ€”instant orders, pre-orders, completed, and pending.

### Total Pokecoins purchased

The cumulative amount of Pokecoins you have purchased across all your orders. This gives you a quick view of your overall purchase volume.

### Total spent

Your lifetime spending on the platform displayed in your currency. This helps you track your overall investment in Pokecoins.

---

## Recent orders

Below the statistics, you will find a list of your most recent orders.

[IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Close-up of the recent orders section]

### Order information displayed

Each order in the list shows:

- **Order ID**: The unique reference number for the order
- **Pokecoin amount**: How many Pokecoins were purchased
- **Status**: Current state of the order (pending, processing, delivered, etc.)
- **Date**: When the order was placed

### Interacting with orders

Click on any order to view its full details. This takes you to the order details page where you can see:

- Complete order timeline
- Payment information
- Delivery confirmation
- Support options

## Quick actions

The Overview includes quick action buttons for common tasks.

[IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Close-up of quick action buttons]

### Buy Pokecoins

Starts a new purchase. Clicking this button takes you directly to the purchase flow where you can select quantity and complete payment.

### View all orders

Opens the full Orders page where you can see your complete order history with filtering and search options.

---

## Sidebar navigation

The sidebar on the left provides navigation to all dashboard sections.

[IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Close-up of the sidebar navigation]

### Available sections

- **Overview**: The current page (your dashboard homepage)
- **Orders**: Complete order history and management
- **Trends**: Analytics and statistics about your purchases
- **Settings**: Account preferences and notification options
- **Notifications**: Updates about your orders and account

### Mobile navigation

On mobile devices, the sidebar collapses into a menu icon. Tap this icon to reveal the navigation options.

[IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Mobile view showing collapsed sidebar and menu icon]
