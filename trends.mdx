The Trends section provides analytics about your purchasing activity on Right Spot. Use these insights to understand your spending patterns and track your Pokecoin acquisitions over time.

## Accessing trends

Navigate to **Trends** in the sidebar to view your analytics dashboard.

[IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Trends analytics view]

## Available metrics

### Purchase volume

A chart displaying the amount of Pokecoins you have purchased over time. You can adjust the time range to view daily, weekly, or monthly aggregations.

This visualization helps you identify patterns in your purchasing behavior and plan future acquisitions.

### Spending summary

A breakdown of your total spending across different time periods:

- **This month**: Spending in the current calendar month
- **Last month**: Spending in the previous calendar month
- **All time**: Total spending since account creation

### Order statistics

Key metrics about your ordering patterns:

- **Average order size**: The typical Pokecoin amount per order
- **Total orders**: Number of orders placed
- **Success rate**: Percentage of orders that completed successfully

### Payment method distribution

A breakdown showing which payment methods you use most frequently. This can help you identify your preferred payment option and understand any patterns in payment method selection.

## Time range selection

At the top of the Trends page, you can select the time range for displayed data:

- **7 days**: Recent activity for the past week
- **30 days**: Activity over the past month
- **90 days**: Quarterly view
- **All time**: Complete account history

Charts and statistics update automatically when you change the time range.

## Using trends data

The trends section is designed to give you visibility into your account activity. Some practical uses include:

**Budget tracking**: Monitor your spending to stay within a personal budget for Poketwo activities.

**Purchase planning**: Identify when you typically need to purchase more Pokecoins and plan accordingly.

**Bulk purchase decisions**: If you notice frequent small purchases, consider larger bulk orders to take advantage of volume discounts.
