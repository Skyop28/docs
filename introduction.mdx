Right Spot is a platform for purchasing Pokecoins for the Poketwo Discord bot. This documentation covers everything from creating your account to tracking your orders and understanding our security practices.

## What you can do with Right Spot

Right Spot provides a streamlined purchasing experience with multiple payment options, real-time order tracking, and instant delivery to your Discord account. Whether you are making your first purchase or managing bulk orders, this documentation will guide you through every step.

## Before you begin

To purchase Pokecoins from Right Spot, you need:

- A Discord account
- A Poketwo market ID of the amount of pokecoins you want to purchase
- A valid payment method (PayPal, credit/debit card, UPI, or cryptocurrency)

If you are new to Poketwo or need help finding your market ID, the ordering guide includes detailed instructions.
